Gym Rules & Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our priority. Please read and follow these guidelines.

Emergency Contact:

In case of emergency, immediately contact gym staff or call: +880-1XXX-XXXXXX

Warm-Up First

Always start with 5-10 minutes of warm-up exercises

Stay Hydrated

Bring water and drink before, during, and after workouts

Use Proper Form

Focus on correct technique over lifting heavy weights

Ask for Help

Request assistance when using new equipment

General Gym Rules

1

Valid Student ID Required

All members must present their valid CUET student ID card upon entry. Guest access is not permitted.

2

Operating Hours

The gym operates from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM (Monday to Saturday). Closed on Sundays and public holidays.

3

Proper Attire

Wear appropriate gym attire and closed-toe athletic shoes. Tank tops, sandals, and barefoot training are prohibited.

4

Personal Hygiene

Maintain good personal hygiene. Use towels on equipment and clean machines after use with provided sanitizing wipes.

5

Equipment Return

Return all weights, dumbbells, and equipment to their designated places after use. Re-rack weights properly.

6

No Dropping Weights

Avoid dropping or slamming weights. Control the weight during both lifting and lowering phases.

7

Share Equipment

Allow others to work in between your sets during busy periods. Time limits may apply on cardio equipment (30 minutes).

8

Mobile Phone Use

Keep phone conversations brief and outside the workout areas. Use headphones for music at reasonable volume.

9

No Food in Gym Area

Food is not allowed on the gym floor. Water and sports drinks are permitted in secure bottles only.

10

Respect Others

Be courteous to fellow members and staff. Harassment, offensive language, and inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated.

Safety Guidelines

Medical Clearance

Consult a healthcare provider before starting any new exercise program, especially if you have existing medical conditions.

Know Your Limits

Don't push beyond your capabilities. Stop immediately if you feel dizzy, nauseous, or experience chest pain.

Use Spotters

Always use a spotter when lifting heavy weights, especially for bench press and squats.

Learn Proper Technique

Ask trainers or use AI instructor for guidance on proper form before using unfamiliar equipment.

Check Equipment

Inspect equipment before use. Report any damaged or malfunctioning equipment to staff immediately.

Rest & Recovery

Allow adequate rest between workouts. Overtraining can lead to injury and burnout.

Injury Prevention Tips

Always warm up before starting your workout and cool down afterwards

Progress gradually - increase weight, intensity, or duration slowly over time

Maintain proper posture and form during all exercises

Wear appropriate footwear with good support and grip

Stay focused - avoid distractions while exercising

Listen to your body - pain is a warning sign, not something to ignore

Equipment Usage Guidelines

Free Weights

  • Start with lighter weights to master form
  • Use collars to secure weight plates
  • Never walk behind someone lifting
  • Place weights down gently

Machines

  • Adjust seat and settings before starting
  • Follow posted instructions
  • Use controlled movements
  • Wipe down after use

Cardio Equipment

  • Start at slow pace and gradually increase
  • Use safety clips on treadmills
  • Limit sessions to 30 minutes during peak hours
  • Step off carefully when finished

Functional Training

  • Ensure adequate space around you
  • Use mats for floor exercises
  • Keep training area clear
  • Return equipment after use

Prohibited Activities

Bringing food onto the gym floor
Using equipment while under the influence of alcohol or drugs
Smoking or vaping inside the facility
Photography or videography without permission
Monopolizing equipment for extended periods
Harassment or intimidation of other members

Rules Acknowledgment

By using the CUET Gymnasium, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to follow all rules and safety guidelines. Violation of these rules may result in suspension of gym privileges.
